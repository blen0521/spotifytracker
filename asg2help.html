About This App

This app helps you track your listening behavior from Spotify and Last.fm.

  • Home: Shows your user profile and listening summary.
  • Dashboard: Displays your top artists and tracks using Spotify data.
  • User Plays: Gives you detailed scrobbles and weekly trends from Last.fm.
  • Troubleshooting: Ensure you are logged into Spotify and Last.fm when connecting.
